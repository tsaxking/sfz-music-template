sfzMusic: Policies

TLDR:

  • Your Data

    • We will not share your data with any external source

    • We will not spam your email; all emails have a functional use for the site

    • All cookies are limited to being strictly necessary

    • Your passwords are encrypted to the latest industry standard

  • Uploading Files

    • You must have the copyright owner's permission to upload and share content on this site. Your account will be banned if this is infringed

    • Your files are private; only you/your institution have access to the files you/your institution uploads

    • If you upload a file that breaks the law, your account and IP address will be permanently banned and all your information will be reported to the appropriate authorities.

All data, including your account/institution data, belongs to you and we will not share it with any external source. Your data is safely stored on a database and is only accessible to you or to admins if necessary. We do not send email promotions to you; by signing up, you are not agreeing to a subscription based marketing campaign. All emails sent by sfzMusic are from info@sfzmusic.org, if you receive emails from a similar source and it seems like spam, please report it to us by that email. All passwords are encrypted using an iterative hashing algorithm up to the industry standard of encryption. If you suspect your password has been compromised, please contact us immediately. We do not store your password in plain text, if you lose your password, you will need to reset it as it is impossible to decrypt it.

Uploading files that you do not have the copyright owner's permission is only allowed under the condition that you do not use sfzMusic.org to share these files with anyone. Including the Institution and Ensemble Management services. If you do not have the copyright owner's permission and have uploaded files onto here, they can only be there for the sole purpose of an archival backup or educational use. Copyright law states that you cannot do any performance without original music without the permission of the copyright owner. Meaning if you use Ensemble Management pdf sharing services and you perform with the music you do not have the copyright for or its owners permission, you have broken the law.

If you suspect anyone has uploaded files that violate the law, please contact us immediately. We will remove the files from the site and report the incident to the appropriate authorities. You may not upload files that are in these categories:

  • Defamation

  • Fraud

  • Pornography

  • Obscenities

  • Hate Speech

We reserve the right to remove any file or any account that we feel violates the law or our terms of service. We do not have to provide any reason for the removal.

All payments are through Stripe.com, a third party payment processor. We do not store any card information on our servers, as a result, we do not have access to your card information. If you use the student management service and you wish to collect automated payments through sfzMusic, you must complete the tax information found on the Instructor Account page under Lessons in your dashboard. Similar to your card information, all data is stored on Stripe's servers and we do not have access to it. If you have any questions about your payments, please do not hesitate to contact us at info@sfzmusic.org.

Stripe's basic business model is 2.9% + $0.30 per successful transaction. All subscriptions to sfzMusic include this fee so you will not be billed more than the price listed on the site. For instructors, if you choose to use sfzMusic's automatic payments service, each transaction will be charged the 2.9% + $0.30 to offset the cost of Stripe. It is recommended you add this to the cost of your lessons to cover this fee (this is calculated on the site, all it takes is a checkbox). This will be no extra cost to you as it will be now added to the price of your lesson. If you do not wish to use this service, you can still simply collect payments through your own method!

To learn more information of Stripe's privacy policy, please visit https://stripe.com/us/privacy.

We use cookies for functionality only. We do not use tracking cookies or any third party cookies besides those required for Stripe (read Payments). If you see those on your browser, check if you have a password manager (avast, google, logmeonce, etc.), or similar services you have added as extensions and ensure they are from a trusted source. If they are not and you believe they are originating from sfzMusic, please contact us immediately.
